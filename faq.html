    Frequently Asked Questions

    Here are some frequently asked questions that many people may find useful when using this application, Click each to view the answer.


    Is it possible to make an appointment at any time of day?

    Yes. You can make appointment anytime. However vaccination is processing on 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

    Can i use this to make an Appointment for my family using my Account?

    Yes. You can book upto 4 appointments once, including for your family.

    How will I get the details of my appointment?

    You will recive an SMS with a unique Authentication Number.

    I have got my 1st dose already, Can I book 2nd dose of my vaccine?

    Yes. You can book the second dose if it's available in our centers.

    Is there a possibility of appointment cancelation?

    Yes. You can cancel your appointment anytime.

    Is my personal information secure?

    Yes. Your personal information will be securly handled.

    Is any other vaccines avaiable rather than for COVID-19?

    Yes. But right now we have given more priority to COVID-19 vaccinations.

    How will I check if the vaccine is available in particular center?

    You can check the vaccine avalibility of the center before making an appointment.
